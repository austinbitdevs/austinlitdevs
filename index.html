LitDevs is a community for those interested in discussing and participating in the research and development of Bitcoin, Lightning, and related protocols.

We host monthly socratic seminars that are highly technical in nature, and open to all.

We observe the Chatham House rule - "participants are free to use the information received, but neither the identity nor the affiliation of the speaker(s), nor that of any other participant, may be revealed."

NOTE: This socratic seminar series is focused on the Lightning network. For the main general Bitcoin socratic seminar series, see BitDevs.
